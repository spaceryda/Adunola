Rice and Beans recipe

Rice and beans is a nigerian staple food that is simple,
delicious, and very easy to cook. Rice and Beans are a classic combination.
As the name implies, it's rice cooked together with beans, it's inexpensive,
nutritious, and goes with just about any meals.

List of Ingredients and Measurement

Ingredients Measurement
Rice 2 cups
Beans 1 cup
Onion 1 small diced
Salt 1 tablespoon
Butter 1 tablespoon
Water 3 cups

    Instructions

  1. Pick your beans to get rid of any dirt
  2. Rinse the beans and transfer it to a pot, add the diced Onion and water, cover it up, and leave it to cook for about 40 to 50 minutes.
  3. Rinse the rice thoroughly until the water comes out clear stir in the rice, add butter and salt with the beans.
  4. Return to a boil, reduce heat to medium low, cover and cook for 20 minutes without removing the lid.
  5. Fold the rice and beans together gently and serve with your desired sauce, soup, or stew. Enjoy!
